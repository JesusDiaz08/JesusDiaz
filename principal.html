"Poetry goes on the street"

Works like "What I talk about when I talk about running" - Haruki Murakami, "The unbearable lightness of being" - Milan Kundera, "The intermittences of death" - José Saramago, are among the most recent in my day to day as a person. Currently I write small fragments of poetry and my day to day.
